## Scenario
1. Switch 1: Default to `Turn on` the switch 1. `Turn on` and `Turn off` via MQTT message.
1. Switch 2: Default to `Turn off` the switch 2. `Turn on` and `Turn off` via MQTT message.

## Hardware & PIN IN / PIN OUT connection
1. ESP32-C3 Super Mini
2. Relay 1: digital out (GPIO 2)
2. Relay 2: digital out (GPIO 3)
